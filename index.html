Last updated: April 2026

1. Who we are

IconAI ("we", "us", "our") is an AI-powered graphic generation service. We help users create SVG icons, illustrations, infographics, and images. Our contact email is hello@iconai.app.

2. What data we collect

Account data: When you sign up, we collect your email address and a hashed password. If you sign in via Google or another OAuth provider, we receive your name and email from that provider.

Usage data: We log the number of generations you perform per month to enforce plan limits. We do not store the text prompts you enter or the images you generate on our servers.

API keys: Any API keys you enter (Anthropic, OpenAI, fal.ai) are stored in your browser's local storage only. They are never transmitted to or stored on our servers.

Payment data: Billing is handled entirely by Stripe. We never see or store your card number. We receive a Stripe customer ID and subscription status from Stripe.

Analytics: We use privacy-respecting analytics (no cross-site tracking, no fingerprinting) to understand page traffic and feature usage in aggregate.

We use your data solely to: provide and improve the service, enforce usage limits on your plan, send transactional emails (receipts, password resets), and respond to support requests. We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising.

4. Third-party services

When you generate content, your prompts are sent directly from your browser to the AI provider you have configured (Anthropic, OpenAI, fal.ai). Each provider has its own privacy policy. We recommend reviewing them:

  • Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI Privacy Policy
  • fal.ai Privacy Policy
  • Stripe Privacy Policy

5. Data retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we delete your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law (e.g. billing records, which Stripe retains for 7 years).

6. Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data at any time. To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@iconai.app. We will respond within 30 days.

If you are in the EU or UK, you have additional rights under GDPR / UK GDPR, including the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

7. Cookies

We use only essential cookies necessary for authentication (keeping you logged in) and session management. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

8. Children

IconAI is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy occasionally. When we do, we will update the date at the top and notify registered users by email of material changes. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance.

10. Contact
